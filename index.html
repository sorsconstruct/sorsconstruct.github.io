What is vibration isolator in HVAC?

Vibration isolation is basically the process of isolating vibrating parts from the rest of a machine or system so that these parts don't cause vibration and noise in other parts of the machine. This type of vibration control is incredibly important in HVAC systems for several reasons.

  • Vibration isolation prevents vibration transmission.
  • It keeps vibration energy from entering an object, such as a structure or piece of equipment.
  • Vibration damping dissipates vibration energy.

It absorbs or changes vibration energy, reducing the amount of energy transmitted through the equipment or structure.
